Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy describes how I collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with my portfolio website. I am committed to being transparent about my data practices while ensuring your privacy.

Data Collection

I collect the following information when you visit my website:

  • Visitor Identifier: A randomly generated unique ID (UUID) to track your interactions
  • Location Data: City and country information through your browser's geolocation
  • Basic Browser Info:
    • Browser language preference
    • Timezone
    • Browser type (user agent)
  • Interaction Data:
    • Flowers sent
    • Project feedback (likes/dislikes)
    • Visit timestamps
  • Contact Form Data (only if you message me):
    • Name
    • Email address
    • Message content
    • Newsletter preference

Technical Infrastructure

My website uses the following technologies:

  • Database: Supabase (secure, PostgreSQL-based database service)
  • Data Storage: Data is stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Frontend: HTML, JavaScript, and TailwindCSS

How I Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying interactive elements (flower counts, feedback)
  • Generating anonymous engagement statistics
  • Improving website experience
  • Responding to your messages (if you contact me)
  • Sending newsletters (only if you opt-in)

Data Privacy & Security

I protect your data through:

  • Secure data storage using Supabase's encrypted infrastructure
  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Limited data retention periods
  • Minimal data collection - I only collect what's necessary
  • No sharing of personal data with third parties

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to know what data I collect
  • Right to request data deletion
  • Right to opt-out of data collection (through your browser settings)
  • Right to unsubscribe from newsletters
  • Right to contact me about your data

Data Retention

I retain interaction data (flowers, feedback) for website functionality and analytics purposes. Contact form submissions are retained for communication purposes and are deleted upon request.
